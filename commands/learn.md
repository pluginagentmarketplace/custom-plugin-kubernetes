## Interactive Selection Process

1. **Choose Your Career Path**
   - Select from 65+ roles across different specializations
   - Categories include: Frontend, Backend, DevOps, Data/AI, Mobile, Management, Security, and more

2. **Assess Your Current Level**
   - Beginner: Just starting your journey
   - Intermediate: Have foundational knowledge
   - Advanced: Looking to deepen expertise

3. **Define Your Goals**
   - Job transition
   - Skill expansion
   - Technology specialization
   - Full-stack mastery

4. **Get Your Learning Plan**
   - Personalized step-by-step roadmap
   - Estimated time to completion
   - Recommended projects and resources
   - Skill assessment milestones
